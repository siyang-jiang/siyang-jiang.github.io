Biography

Hello! I am a 4-th year PhD student in Information Engineering at The Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK), under the supervision of Prof. Guoliang Xing . Before joining CUHK, I obtained my M.S. in Electronic Engineering at National Taiwan University (NTU) in 2021, supervised by Prof. Ming-Syan Chen .

My research focuses on Trustworthy and Efficient AI-powered IoT systems (AIoT) for healthcare applications. I aim to develop trustworthy, efficient, and secure AI solutions that can be deployed on resource-constrained IoT devices while protecting sensitive health data.

My work aims to create AI systems that are not only theoretically sound but also practically deployable in healthcare, IoT, and other mission-critical applications where privacy, efficiency, and reliability are paramount.
